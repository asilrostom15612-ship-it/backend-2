01

Overview

At Practical College Tools, we stand behind the quality of our laboratory equipment and scientific instruments. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return most items within 30 days of delivery.

All returns must be in original condition, with all accessories, manuals, and packaging included.

02

Eligibility for Returns

✅

Item is defective or damaged

Upon arrival or within the warranty period

📦

Wrong item received

We will arrange a free replacement or refund

🔄

Change of mind

For most standard items (restocking fee may apply for large equipment)

🎓

Academic bulk orders

Special return conditions may apply – contact us

03

How to Return an Item

1

Contact Us First

Email returns@practicaltools.com or call us with your order number and reason for return.

2

Receive Return Approval

We will provide you with a Return Authorization (RA) number and shipping instructions.

3

Ship the Item Back

Pack the item securely in its original packaging and ship it to the address provided.

4

Receive Refund or Replacement

Once we receive and inspect the item, we will process your refund or send a replacement.

04

Refunds

Refund Timeline

  • Once inspected: 2–5 business days
  • Original payment method: 5–10 business days

What is Refunded

• Full item price (if eligible)
• Shipping cost (only if the return is due to our error)

• Return shipping fees are paid by the customer unless the item is defective or incorrect.

05

Non-Returnable Items

• Opened chemicals and reagents

• Used or installed glassware & consumables

• Customized or engraved equipment

• Perishable or time-sensitive materials

• Items marked as “Final Sale” or “Non-Returnable”

• Special bulk orders for universities (case by case)
